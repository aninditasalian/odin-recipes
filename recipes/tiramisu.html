Why you should eat Tiramisu

Tiramisu cake uses cocoa powder, which contains flavonoids, plant materials recognised for their heart-protective effects. Flavonoids have been linked to enhanced blood circulation and a lower risk of cardiovascular illnesses. A lower risk of heart disease has also been associated with moderate coffee consumption.
